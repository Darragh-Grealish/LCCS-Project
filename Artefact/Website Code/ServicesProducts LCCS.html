Easy Room Monitoring System

This Low Cost system aims to imporve your safety, security and general wellbeing. Protecting your home from environmental damages such as fire and smoke.

The embedded system along with an OLED display, 2 leds (red and blue), a dust sensor, flame sensor and temperature sensor, gathers information on the rooms temperature, dust levels and any flame detected. It knows when the door is closed detecting when the magnet stuck to the door is near, using it's built in magnetic detection system.

Its known that keeping the doors closed in a house can stop the spread of any fire that may occur in a particular room. Thats why our system monitors the rooms environment and the door. This information can be easily gotten on our website on the Monitor Your Device page. Just by checking your smartphone you can easily know which doors are opened and closed before you go to bed.

The Easy Room Monitoring System aims to provide safety and convienve to those who own one. Smart-Home Technology isn't cheap and neither is home insurance, so here at Forged Security we decided to create a cheap way of allowing people to have their own piece of smart-home technology whilst reducing their home insurance costs.
